## shadcn/ui themes

Customize your [shadcn/ui](https://github.com/shadcn-ui/ui) theme that you can copy and paste into your apps. Free and Open Source. Use this to extract maximum power of shadcn/ui.

![hero](public/og.png)

## Development stages
